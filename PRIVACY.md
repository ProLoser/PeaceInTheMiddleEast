# Privacy Policy

**backgammon.family** is a free, open-source backgammon game. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Information We Collect

When you sign in, we store only the following information:

- **Display name** – used to identify you to friends and opponents.
- **Profile photo** – used as your avatar in the game.

No other personal information is collected, stored, or shared.

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

- Email addresses (even if used for sign-in)
- Phone numbers (even if used for sign-in)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Browsing history or behavior analytics

## How Your Information Is Used

Your display name and profile photo are stored solely to enable the multiplayer experience — so other players can find and recognize you.

## Data Deletion

You may change your display name at any time from within the app. Signing out or deleting your account removes your session data.

## Contact

This is an open-source project. If you have privacy concerns, please [open an issue](https://github.com/ProLoser/PeaceInTheMiddleEast/issues/new) on GitHub.
